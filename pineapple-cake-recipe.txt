Cake recipe easy pineapple
Preparation time: 15 minutes
Cooking time: 45 minutes
Ingredients (serves 8): - 500 g of sliced pineapple, canned
- 4 eggs
- 250 g caster sugar
- 150 g butter
- 250g of flour
- 3 cl rum
- 1 lemon
- 1 packet of dry yeast
For the caramel:
- 125 g of sugar
- 4 tablespoons water


+++++++++++++++++

Drain the pineapple slices and keep the syrup. Prepare caramel.
Pour into a cake pan, well coat the bottom. Preheat oven to 180 Â° C.
Ask the caramel pineapple slices to cover the bottom of pan. Place the remaining slices in a blender.
Whisk the eggs with the sugar foamy, add the softened butter and cut in pieces, flour, half rum, lemon juice, baking powder and puree pineapple.
Mix well and pour into the mold.
Bake 45 to 50 min. Let cool slightly before turning and watering the cake from the rest of rum and pineapple syrup.
